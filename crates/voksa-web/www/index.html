voksa — Lojban speech tuning console

Everything runs in your browser. Fiddle the knobs, listen, then Download config and share the JSON — it replays exactly via voksa --config file.json.

cnima'o — attitudinals. A UI cmavo colors the word before it with an emotion (coi munje .ui = a joyful munje). Follow it with nai to flip the emotion, sai/ru'e to weaken it. These deviation vectors are voksa's own invention (the CLL defines meaning, not sound) — tune them and share your config JSON.

lo voksa selci — the phoneme table. Every acoustic parameter of every phoneme: vowel formants (karsna), consonant loci (zunsna), noise bands, voicing, durations. Diphthong glides follow the tuned vowels; ' ([h]) shapes itself from the next vowel. Retune the voice itself, then share the config JSON.
